NFTs (Non-Fungible Tokens) can be used in the car resale market to create a digital record of a vehicle's ownership history, maintenance records, and other important details. This can help to establish the provenance of a car, which is a key factor in determining its resale value. By using NFTs to create a digital record of a car's ownership history and other details, the car resale market can become more transparent and efficient. This can benefit both buyers and sellers by making it easier to establish the true value of a vehicle.
